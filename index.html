Chris Lipscomb

An aspiring web and software developer at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute.

I am a back-end and front-end web developer, software programmer, and junior at RPI. I have been developing web sites since I was in high school, and I am very passionate about creating high quality products that users will enjoy.


While I work a lot with back-end development, I also have a lot of experience with front-end development and design. The functionality must be there, but the design of a web page or application is critical for making it usable, for functionality is nothing without usability.


I spend a lot of my free time working on personal projects, such as a tournament system for the online community game Mega Man Battle Network Cyber Tournament. I also have several interesting projects from my time at school; for example, I worked with a group of RPI students to help a local Troy craft ice cream store.
